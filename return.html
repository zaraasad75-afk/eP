Returns Policy

Our Returns Policy

At CERA Tiles, all purchases are in-store only. We do not offer online ordering or deliveries.

If you wish to return or exchange a product, please visit our store with your receipt. Our team will assist you with the process and provide guidance on suitable alternatives if needed.

Returns or exchanges are accepted within a reasonable timeframe, and products must be in their original condition and packaging. Personalized or cut tiles may not be eligible for returns.

For any questions regarding returns, feel free to speak with our store staff. We are committed to ensuring you are satisfied with your purchase.
